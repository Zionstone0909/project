One might draw inspiration from verses that highlight the importance of equipping individuals with the virtues of courage, wisdom, and resilience. In a broader sense, biblical teachings often emphasize the development of character, integrity, and faith, which are essential elements in building a mighty force. Proverbs 20:29 states, "The glory of young men is their strength, gray hair the splendor of the old." This implies a continuum of strength and wisdom passed through generations. Therefore, building a mighty army biblically involves not only the physical aspects but also the spiritual and moral development of individuals, fostering qualities that contribute to strength, resilience, and righteousness.
